Dining on Taniti

Taniti offers a variety of dining options to satisfy every palate. From local delicacies to international cuisine, our island has something for everyone.

Local Cuisine

Five of our restaurants serve traditional Tanitian dishes, focusing on fresh, locally-caught fish and locally-grown produce.

American-Style Restaurants

For those craving familiar flavors, we have three restaurants offering American-style meals.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two of our restaurants specialize in Pan-Asian cuisine, offering a fusion of flavors from across Asia.

Grocery and Convenience Stores

For self-catering options, Taniti has:

  • Two supermarkets
  • Two smaller grocery stores
  • One convenience store (open 24 hours a day)
