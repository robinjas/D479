Rental Cars on Taniti

Exploring Taniti Island by car gives you the freedom to discover all our beautiful locations at your own pace. Our local rental agency offers a variety of vehicles to suit your needs.

Available Rental Options

  • Compact cars
  • SUVs
  • Luxury vehicles
  • Electric cars

Rental Process

To rent a car on Taniti:

  1. Visit our rental agency near the airport
  2. Present a valid driver's license and credit card
  3. Choose your preferred vehicle
  4. Review and sign the rental agreement
  5. Enjoy your drive around Taniti!

Driving Tips

  • Drive on the left side of the road
  • Observe posted speed limits
  • Be cautious on rural roads, which may be narrower
  • Always wear your seatbelt
  • Do not drink and drive

For more information about transportation options on Taniti, please visit our Transportation Overview page.
