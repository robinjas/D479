Transportation on Taniti

Getting to Taniti

Most visitors arrive to Taniti by air. We are also served by a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once a week.

Airport

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. We are in the process of expanding the airport to accommodate larger jets within the next few years.

Public Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City.

Car Rentals

Rental cars are available from a local rental agency near the airport. For more information, visit our Rental Cars page.

Bicycles

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Please note that helmets are required by law.

Walking

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. The area surrounding Merriton Landing is particularly easy to explore on foot.
