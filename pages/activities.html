Activities on Taniti

Beaches

Enjoy our pristine sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports.

Hiking

Explore our lush rainforests and mountainous interior. Don't miss the opportunity to hike to our small, active volcano.

Water Activities

Go on chartered fishing tours, enjoy snorkeling in our vibrant coral reefs, or try exciting water sports.

Cultural Experiences

Visit our local history museum to learn about Taniti's rich heritage. Immerse yourself in local traditions and customs.

Other Activities

  • Zip-lining through the rainforest
  • Visiting local pubs and our new microbrewery
  • Dancing at our new dance club
  • Watching movies at our cinema
  • Taking scenic helicopter rides
  • Playing at our arcade
  • Visiting art galleries
  • Bowling

Coming soon: A nine-hole golf course, expected to be operational by next year.
