Lodging on Taniti

Taniti offers a wide variety of accommodations to suit every preference and budget. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure high standards of quality and safety.

Family-Owned Hotels

Bed and Breakfasts

Enjoy a cozy, home-like atmosphere at our growing number of bed and breakfast establishments.
